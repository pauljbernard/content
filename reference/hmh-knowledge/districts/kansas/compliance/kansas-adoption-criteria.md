# Kansas Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Education Requirements**
**Source:** Kansas State Department of Education (KSDE)
**Applies To:** All Kansas instructional materials

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | Kansas |
|--------|--------|
| **Content Standards** | Kansas Academic Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Science Standards** | Kansas Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | Kansas Assessment Program (KAP) |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## Kansas Academic Standards

**Mathematics:** KS Math Standards (based on CCSS-M, 95%+ aligned)
**ELA:** KS ELA Standards (based on CCSS-ELA)
**Science:** KS Science Standards (based on NGSS)
