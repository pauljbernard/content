# Clark County School District Adoption Criteria
**District:** Clark County School District (CCSD)
**State:** Nevada
**Students:** 320,000 (fifth largest US)
**Applies To:** All K-8 instructional materials

---

## Overview

Clark County (Las Vegas area) serves 320,000 students, Nevada's only large district. Rapidly growing with high ELL population and desert environment.

**See:** `/districts/nevada/compliance/nevada-adoption-criteria.md` for NV requirements (if exists, 90% reuse)

Clark County follows Nevada state standards (CCSS-aligned) with district priorities.

---

## Clark County Priorities

### 1. English Learner Success (60,000+ ELLs, 160+ languages)
**Top Languages:** Spanish, Tagalog, Chinese, Vietnamese
- 19% ELL enrollment (high percentage)
- WIDA framework
- Newcomer programs
- Bilingual supports

### 2. Rapid Growth and Mobility
- High student mobility
- Rapid population growth
- Diverse newcomer populations (domestic and international)
- Transitioning students support

### 3. Desert/Southwest Context
- Desert ecosystems and conservation
- Water resources and sustainability
- Western US geography
- Las Vegas economy (tourism, hospitality, entertainment, technology)

### 4. College and Career Readiness
- Nevada standards aligned to college readiness
- Career Technical Education (CTE)
- Hospitality/tourism careers
- STEM emphasis
