# Learning Community Builder
Build thriving online learning communities that support peer interaction and knowledge sharing.
## When to Use: Online courses, cohort-based learning, professional communities
## Elements: Community guidelines, engagement strategies, moderation, recognition systems
## CLI: /learning.community-builder --platform "discourse" --size "500" --topic "data science"
## Output: Community structure, guidelines, engagement calendar, moderation plan
## Exit Codes: 0=success, 1=invalid platform, 2=unrealistic size
