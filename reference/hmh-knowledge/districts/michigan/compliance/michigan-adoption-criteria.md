# Michigan Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Education Requirements**
**Source:** Michigan Department of Education (MDE)
**Applies To:** All Michigan instructional materials (all subjects)

---

## Overview

**Michigan uses local control for curriculum adoption** with state oversight through MDE. All materials must align to Michigan Academic Standards and support English Learners.

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | Michigan |
|--------|----------|
| **Content Standards** | Michigan Academic Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Compliance** | MDE guidance |
| **Science Standards** | Michigan Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | M-STEP (3-8), MME/SAT (11) |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## Michigan Academic Standards

**Mathematics:** Michigan Academic Standards for Mathematics (based on CCSS-M, 95%+ identical)

**ELA:** Michigan Academic Standards for English Language Arts (based on CCSS-ELA)

**Science:** Michigan Science Standards (based on NGSS)

---

## English Learner Support

**Michigan is a WIDA consortium member:**
- WIDA ELD Standards
- ACCESS for ELLs
- 6 proficiency levels

**See:** `/districts/illinois/language/illinois-el-standards.md` for complete WIDA guidance
