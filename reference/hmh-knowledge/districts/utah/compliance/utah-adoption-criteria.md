# Utah Adoption Criteria
**State Board of Education Requirements | Source:** Utah SBE | **Applies To:** All Utah instructional materials

## Key Facts
| Aspect | Utah |
|--------|------|
| **Standards** | Utah Core Standards - CCSS-based | **Language** | State EL standards + WIDA ACCESS |
| **Adoption** | Local district control | **Science** | Utah Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | RISE (Reading, Integrated Science, English) | **EL Assessment** | WIDA ACCESS |

## Utah Core Standards
**Math/ELA:** Based on CCSS-M/CCSS-ELA (90%+ aligned) | **Science:** NGSS-based

## English Learner Support
**Utah has state-specific EL standards** but uses WIDA ACCESS assessment (similar to Colorado).
**See:** `/districts/utah/language/utah-el-standards.md`

## Knowledge Reuse (95%+)
**Reused:** CCSS-M, NGSS, MLRs, universal frameworks
