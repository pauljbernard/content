# Learning Collaborative Project
Structure effective group projects that develop collaboration skills and shared knowledge.
## When to Use: Project-based learning, team assignments, collaborative research
## Elements: Clear roles, scaffolding, individual accountability, team rubrics, conflict protocols
## CLI: /learning.collaborative-project --topic "sustainable design" --team-size "4" --duration "4 weeks"
## Output: Project brief, role definitions, milestones, team rubric, facilitation guide
## Exit Codes: 0=success, 1=invalid team size, 2=duration too short
