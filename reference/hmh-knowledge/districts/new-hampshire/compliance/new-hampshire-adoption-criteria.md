# New Hampshire Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Education Requirements**
**Source:** New Hampshire DOE
**Applies To:** All New Hampshire instructional materials

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | New Hampshire |
|--------|---------------|
| **Content Standards** | NH Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Science Standards** | NH Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | NH SAS (Statewide Assessment System) |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## New Hampshire Standards

**Mathematics:** NH Math Standards (based on CCSS-M, 95%+ aligned)
**ELA:** NH ELA Standards (based on CCSS-ELA)
**Science:** NH Science Standards (based on NGSS)
