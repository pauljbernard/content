# Delaware Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Education Requirements**
**Source:** Delaware DOE
**Applies To:** All Delaware instructional materials

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | Delaware |
|--------|----------|
| **Content Standards** | DE Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Science Standards** | DE Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | DeSSA (Delaware System of Student Assessments) |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## Delaware Standards

**Mathematics:** DE Math Standards (based on CCSS-M, 95%+ aligned)
**ELA:** DE ELA Standards (based on CCSS-ELA)
**Science:** DE Science Standards (based on NGSS)
