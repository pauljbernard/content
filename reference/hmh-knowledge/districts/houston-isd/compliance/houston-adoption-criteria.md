# Houston Independent School District Adoption Criteria
**District:** Houston ISD
**State:** Texas
**Students:** 200,000 (largest in Texas, seventh in US)
**Applies To:** All K-8 instructional materials

---

## Overview

Houston ISD is the largest district in Texas, serving 200,000 students in the nation's fourth-largest city. Exceptionally diverse with 75+ languages and majority Hispanic/Latino enrollment.

**Key Principle:** Meet Texas state standards (TEKS, ELPS) AND Houston ISD district requirements.

---

## Relationship to Texas State

**See:** `/districts/texas/compliance/texas-adoption-criteria.md` for full Texas requirements (TEKS, ELPS, SBOE, IPACC)

Houston ISD follows all Texas state standards plus district-specific priorities.

---

## Houston ISD Priorities

### 1. Multilingual Education (65,000+ ELLs, 75+ languages)

**Largest Languages:** Spanish, Arabic, Vietnamese, Chinese, Urdu

**Programs:**
- Bilingual Education (Spanish/English, Vietnamese/English)
- ESL (English as Second Language)
- Dual Language (two-way immersion, 40+ schools)
- Newcomer programs

**Materials Must:**
- Provide ELPS scaffolds for all 4 proficiency levels
- Include Spanish/English bilingual glossaries
- Support diverse linguistic backgrounds
- Culturally responsive to Houston's diversity

---

### 2. College and Career Readiness

**Every child, every classroom, every day**

**K-8 Focus:**
- Rigorous academics aligned to TEKS
- Critical thinking and problem-solving
- Career exploration (Houston industries: energy, healthcare, aerospace, technology)
- Growth mindset

---

### 3. Houston Context

**Connect to Local Environment:**
- Gulf Coast ecosystem
- Hurricane preparedness
- Space exploration (NASA Johnson Space Center)
- Energy sector (oil, gas, renewables)

**Connect to Local Culture:**
- Diverse international communities
- Immigration histories
- Houston as energy capital
- Healthcare and medical center
