# Wisconsin Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Public Instruction Requirements**
**Source:** Wisconsin DPI
**Applies To:** All Wisconsin instructional materials

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | Wisconsin |
|--------|-----------|
| **Content Standards** | Wisconsin Academic Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium - WIDA headquarters) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Science Standards** | Wisconsin Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | Forward Exam (3-8) |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

**Note:** Wisconsin hosts WIDA headquarters at University of Wisconsin-Madison
