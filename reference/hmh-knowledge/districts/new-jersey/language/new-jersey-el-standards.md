# New Jersey English Learner Standards
**WIDA English Language Development Standards**
**Source:** WIDA Consortium / NJ Department of Education
**Applies To:** K-12 (New Jersey)

---

## Overview

**New Jersey is a WIDA consortium member.** All English Learner support uses WIDA ELD Standards, identical to Illinois and 40+ other states.

---

## WIDA in New Jersey

**Assessment:** ACCESS for ELLs (annual English proficiency test)

**Proficiency Levels:** 6 levels (1.0 Entering â†’ 6.0 Reaching)

**ELD Standards:**
- Standard 1: Social and Instructional Language
- Standard 2: Language of Language Arts
- Standard 3: Language of Mathematics
- Standard 4: Language of Science
- Standard 5: Language of Social Studies

## Quick Reference

**Levels 1-2 (Entering/Emerging):** Heavy scaffolds - visuals, sentence frames, bilingual support

**Levels 3-4 (Developing/Expanding):** Moderate scaffolds - graphic organizers, vocabulary support

**Levels 5-6 (Bridging/Reaching):** Light scaffolds - academic language refinement
