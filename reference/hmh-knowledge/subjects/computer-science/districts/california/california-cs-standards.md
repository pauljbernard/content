# California Computer Science Standards
**California CS Standards**
**Source:** California SBE
**Applies To:** K-12 Computer Science (California only)

---

## Overview

**California adopted CS Standards in 2018** aligned to CSTA framework with emphasis on equity and access.

---

## CA CS Standards Organization

**Based on CSTA:** California standards directly align to CSTA K-12 CS Standards
**Grade Bands:** Same as CSTA (K-2, 3-5, 6-8, 9-12)

---

## CA-Specific Emphasis

**Equity and Access:** Strong focus on broadening participation
**Integration:** CS concepts integrated across subjects
**ELD Support:** Scaffolds for English Learners in CS

**See:** `/districts/california/language/eld-alignment.md` for EL scaffolds
