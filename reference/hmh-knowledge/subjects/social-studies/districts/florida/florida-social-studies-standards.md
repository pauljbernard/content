# Florida Social Studies Standards
**Next Generation Sunshine State Standards for Social Studies**
**Source:** Florida Department of Education
**Applies To:** K-12 Social Studies (Florida only)

---

## Overview

**Florida has state-specific social studies standards** (NGSSS for Social Studies) with required emphasis on civics, economics, geography, and history.

---

## FL Social Studies Format

**Format:** `SS.Grade.Strand.Standard.Benchmark`
**Example:** `SS.5.C.2.1` = Social Studies, Grade 5, Civics (C), Standard 2, Benchmark 1

---

## Strands:

**A:** American History
**C:** Civics and Government
**E:** Economics
**G:** Geography
**W:** World History

---

## Florida-Specific Requirements

**Civics Literacy:** Required strand in Benchmarks for Excellent Student Thinking (B.E.S.T.) ELA
**Grade 7 Civics:** End-of-course assessment required
**Florida History:** Emphasis in Grade 4

**See:** `/subjects/ela/districts/florida/b-e-s-t-ela-alignment.md` for Civics Literacy integration
