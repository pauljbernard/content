# South Dakota Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Education Requirements**
**Source:** South Dakota Department of Education (SDDOE)
**Applies To:** All South Dakota instructional materials

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | South Dakota |
|--------|--------------|
| **Content Standards** | SD Content Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Science Standards** | SD Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | SD State Assessment (Smarter Balanced) |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## South Dakota Content Standards

**Mathematics:** SD Math Standards (based on CCSS-M, 95%+ aligned)
**ELA:** SD ELA Standards (based on CCSS-ELA)
**Science:** SD Science Standards (based on NGSS)

---
