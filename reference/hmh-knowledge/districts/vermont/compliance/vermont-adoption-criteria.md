# Vermont Adoption Criteria
**State Agency of Education Requirements**
**Source:** Vermont Agency of Education
**Applies To:** All Vermont instructional materials

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | Vermont |
|--------|---------|
| **Content Standards** | VT Education Quality Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Science Standards** | VT Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | Smarter Balanced (SBAC) |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## Vermont Education Quality Standards

**Mathematics:** VT Math Standards (based on CCSS-M, 95%+ aligned)
**ELA:** VT ELA Standards (based on CCSS-ELA)
**Science:** VT Science Standards (based on NGSS)
