# San Diego Unified School District Adoption Criteria
**District:** San Diego Unified
**State:** California
**Students:** 120,000
**Applies To:** All K-8 instructional materials

---

## Overview

San Diego Unified serves 120,000 students with strong emphasis on multilingual education and ocean/border context.

**See:** `/districts/california/compliance/california-adoption-criteria.md` for CA requirements (90% reuse)

---

## San Diego Unified Priorities

### 1. Multilingual/Multicultural Education (30,000+ ELs, 60+ languages)
**Top Languages:** Spanish, Somali, Arabic, Vietnamese, Tagalog
- CA ELD scaffolds (5 proficiency levels)
- Bilingual programs (Spanish/English, others)
- Newcomer supports
- Cultural responsiveness (Latino, Somali, refugee communities)

### 2. Ocean/Coastal Context
- Marine ecosystems and conservation
- Coastal geography
- Naval/maritime history (Navy presence)
- Climate and environment

### 3. US-Mexico Border Context
- Cross-border connections (Tijuana)
- Immigration and cultural exchange
- Bilingual/bicultural identity
- Border issues and perspectives

### 4. Graduate Success and Equity
- All students college/career ready
- Close achievement gaps
- Equitable access to advanced coursework
