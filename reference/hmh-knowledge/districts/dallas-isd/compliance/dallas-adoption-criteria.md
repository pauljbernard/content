# Dallas Independent School District Adoption Criteria
**District:** Dallas ISD
**State:** Texas
**Students:** 145,000
**Applies To:** All K-8 instructional materials

---

## Overview

Dallas ISD serves 145,000 students with 90%+ students of color and 45,000+ ELLs.

**See:** `/districts/texas/compliance/texas-adoption-criteria.md` for Texas requirements (90% reuse)

---

## Dallas ISD Priorities

### 1. Racial Equity and Excellence
**90%+ students of color** (Hispanic 70%, African American 22%)
- Culturally responsive materials
- Diverse representation
- Anti-bias content
- Asset-based view of communities

### 2. ELL Support (45,000+ ELLs, 90+ languages)
**Top Languages:** Spanish, Arabic, Burmese, Vietnamese
- ELPS scaffolds (4 proficiency levels)
- Bilingual programs (Spanish/English)
- Newcomer centers
- Spanish/Arabic bilingual materials

### 3. Dallas Context
- Dallas history and civil rights
- Urban planning and growth
- Technology sector (Silicon Prairie)
- Arts and culture
