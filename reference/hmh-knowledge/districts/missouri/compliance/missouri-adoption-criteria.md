# Missouri Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Elementary and Secondary Education Requirements**
**Source:** Missouri DESE
**Applies To:** All Missouri instructional materials

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | Missouri |
|--------|----------|
| **Content Standards** | Missouri Learning Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Science Standards** | Missouri Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | MAP (Missouri Assessment Program) |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## Missouri Learning Standards

**Mathematics:** MO Math Standards (based on CCSS-M, 90%+ aligned)
**ELA:** MO ELA Standards (based on CCSS-ELA)
**Science:** MO Science Standards (based on NGSS)
