# Florida Computer Science Integration
**Florida CS Standards and Expectations**
**Source:** Florida Department of Education
**Applies To:** K-12 Computer Science (Florida only)

---

## Overview

**Florida integrates CS concepts** into existing Digital Information Technology (DIT) courses and offers standalone CS courses.

---

## Florida CS Approach

**K-8:** Integrated into Digital Literacy and Computer Science standards
**9-12:** Dedicated CS courses (Foundations of Programming, Computer Science Principles, Computer Science A)

---

## Florida CS Courses

**Middle School:** Digital Information Technology, Coding courses
**High School:** Computer Science pathway aligned to industry certifications

**Industry Certifications:** Florida emphasizes career pathways and certifications

---

## Alignment to CSTA

**Base Framework:** Florida standards align to CSTA concepts (85-90% aligned)
**Career Focus:** Additional emphasis on industry applications and certifications

---
