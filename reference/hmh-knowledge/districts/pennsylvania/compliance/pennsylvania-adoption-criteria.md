# Pennsylvania Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Education Requirements**
**Source:** Pennsylvania Department of Education (PDE)
**Applies To:** All Pennsylvania instructional materials (all subjects)

---

## Overview

**Pennsylvania uses local control for curriculum adoption** with state oversight through PDE. All materials must align to Pennsylvania Core Standards and support English Learners.

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | Pennsylvania |
|--------|--------------|
| **Content Standards** | PA Core Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Compliance** | PDE guidance |
| **Science Standards** | PA Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | PSSA (3-8), Keystone Exams (HS) |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## PA Core Standards

**Mathematics:** PA Core Standards for Mathematics (based on CCSS-M, 95%+ identical)

**ELA:** PA Core Standards for English Language Arts (based on CCSS-ELA)

**Science:** Pennsylvania Science Standards (based on NGSS)

---

## English Learner Support

**Pennsylvania is a WIDA consortium member:**
- WIDA ELD Standards
- ACCESS for ELLs assessment
- 6 proficiency levels (1.0 → 6.0)

**See:** `/districts/illinois/language/illinois-el-standards.md` for complete WIDA guidance

---

## Knowledge Reuse (95%+)

**Reused:** CCSS-M, NGSS, WIDA, MLRs, assessment files, universal frameworks

**PA-Specific:** PA Core branding, Keystone Exam alignment (HS)

---

## Implementation Notes

**Standards:** PA.CCSS.Math.5.NF.A.1 ≈ CCSS.MATH.CONTENT.5.NF.A.1

**Language:** WIDA levels 1-6, Standard 3 (Math) or 4 (Science)

**Assessment:** PSSA (grades 3-8), Keystone Exams (Algebra I, Literature, Biology)
