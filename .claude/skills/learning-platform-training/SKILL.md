# Learning Platform Training
Develop training materials for educators and administrators on educational technology platforms (Canvas, Moodle, etc.).

## CLI: /learning.platform-training --platform 'Canvas' --audience 'teachers' --topics 'grading,assignments,analytics'
## Exit Codes: 0=success, 1=invalid platform, 2=incomplete training
