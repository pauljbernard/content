## Overview

---

## Organization

**Standards:** State-specific codes aligned to C3 Framework dimensions
**Grade Levels:** K-12 with state-specific scope and sequence

---

## C3 Framework Integration

**Washington standards integrate the C3 Inquiry Arc:**
- Dimension 1: Developing Questions and Planning Inquiries
- Dimension 2: Applying Disciplinary Concepts (Civics, Economics, Geography, History)
- Dimension 3: Evaluating Sources and Using Evidence
- Dimension 4: Communicating Conclusions and Taking Informed Action

**See:** `/subjects/social-studies/common/c3-framework-guide.md` for complete C3 Framework guidance
