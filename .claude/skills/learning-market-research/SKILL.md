# Learning Market Research

Research educational market opportunities, competitive landscape, demand signals, and positioning strategies.

## When to Use
- Launching new course/program
- Competitive positioning
- Pricing strategy
- Market validation
- Trend identification

## Key Analyses

### 1. Competitive Course Analysis
- Identify competing offerings
- Feature comparison matrices
- Pricing analysis
- Review/rating analysis
- Market share estimation

### 2. Learner Demand Assessment
- Search volume trends
- Social media signals
- Job market demand
- Skills gap reports
- Enrollment data

### 3. Pricing Research
- Price points by competitor
- Willingness-to-pay surveys
- Value-based pricing models
- Freemium vs. paid analysis

### 4. Technology Landscape
- Learning platform trends
- Emerging ed-tech tools
- Adoption rates
- Integration capabilities

## CLI Interface
```bash
/learning.market-research --topic "data science" --audience "professionals" --region "US"
/learning.market-research --competitive-analysis --competitors "Coursera,Udemy,LinkedIn"
/learning.market-research --demand --keyword "machine learning certification"
```

## Output
- Market opportunity report
- Competitive positioning matrix
- Demand forecasts
- Pricing recommendations

## Composition
**Input to**: `/learning.needs-analysis`, `/curriculum.design`, `/learning.feasibility-study`

## Exit Codes
- **0**: Research complete
- **1**: Insufficient market data
- **2**: Invalid market segment
