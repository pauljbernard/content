# Houston ISD ELL Framework
**District:** Houston ISD
**ELLs:** 65,000+ (32% of enrollment)
**Languages:** 75+, top: Spanish, Arabic, Vietnamese, Chinese, Urdu
**Assessment:** TELPAS (Texas English Language Proficiency Assessment System)

---

## Overview

Houston ISD serves 65,000+ ELLs speaking 75+ languages. Uses Texas ELPS (English Language Proficiency Standards) with district bilingual programs.

**See:** `/districts/texas/language/texas-elps-alignment.md` for full Texas ELPS framework (85% reuse)

---

## ELPS 4 Proficiency Levels (Texas)

1. Beginning
2. Intermediate
3. Advanced
4. Advanced High

---

## Houston ISD Programs

- **Bilingual:** Spanish/English, Vietnamese/English
- **ESL:** English-focused with sheltered instruction
- **Dual Language:** 40+ schools (expanding)
- **Newcomer Centers:** Recently arrived immigrants/refugees

---

## Top Languages

**Translation priorities:**
1. Spanish (majority)
2. Arabic
3. Vietnamese
4. Chinese (Mandarin/Cantonese)
5. Urdu
