# TEKS Social Studies Alignment
**Texas Essential Knowledge and Skills for Social Studies**
**Source:** Texas Education Agency / SBOE
**Applies To:** K-12 Social Studies (Texas only)

---

## Overview

**Texas has state-specific social studies standards (TEKS)** with emphasis on Texas history, government, and economics.

---

## TEKS Social Studies Format

**Format:** `Grade.Strand.Standard`
**Example:** `5.1A` = Grade 5, Strand 1 (History), Standard A

---

## Strands (K-8):

**Strand 1: History**
**Strand 2: Geography**
**Strand 3: Economics**
**Strand 4: Government**
**Strand 5: Citizenship**
**Strand 6: Culture**
**Strand 7: Science, Technology, and Society**
**Strand 8: Social Studies Skills**

---

## Texas-Specific Content

**Texas History:** Required in Grade 7
**Texas Government:** Emphasis in Grade 4, high school
**Celebrate Freedom Week:** Required observance
