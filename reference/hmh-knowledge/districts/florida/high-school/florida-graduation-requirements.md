# Florida High School Graduation Requirements
**State:** Florida
**Applies To:** All Florida high school graduation programs

---

## Overview

Florida offers multiple diploma options: Standard 24-Credit Diploma, Standard 18-Credit Diploma (ACCEL), and Career and Technical Education (CTE) pathways. All programs require passing End-of-Course (EOC) assessments.

---

## 24-Credit Standard Diploma (Most Common)

**English Language Arts:** 4 credits
- English I, II, III, IV
- Must include ELA EOC assessment

**Mathematics:** 4 credits (must include Algebra I and Geometry)
- Algebra I (required, EOC required)
- Geometry (required, EOC required)
- Two additional math courses

**Science:** 3 credits (must include Biology and two lab courses)
- Biology I (required, EOC required)
- Two additional lab science courses (Chemistry, Physics, or other)

**Social Studies:** 3 credits
- World History (1 credit)
- US History (1 credit, EOC required)
- US Government (0.5 credit)
- Economics with Financial Literacy (0.5 credit)

**Fine Arts, Performing Arts, Speech/Debate, or Practical Arts:** 1 credit

**Physical Education:** 1 credit
- Must include integration of health

**Electives:** 8 credits

**Online Course:** 1 credit (must complete at least one course online)

**Total:** 24 credits

---

## 18-Credit ACCEL Diploma (Accelerated Option)

**Reduced Credit Requirements:**
- English: 4 credits
- Math: 4 credits (including Algebra I, Geometry)
- Science: 3 credits (including Biology)
- Social Studies: 3 credits
- Fine/Performing Arts or Practical Arts: 1 credit
- Physical Education: 1 credit
- Electives: 2 credits

**Same EOC Requirements as 24-Credit Diploma**

**Total:** 18 credits

---

## Florida End-of-Course (EOC) Assessments (Required for Graduation)

**5 EOC Exams:**
1. Algebra I (counts 30% of course grade)
2. Geometry (counts 30% of course grade)
3. Biology I (counts 30% of course grade)
4. US History (counts 30% of course grade)
5. *Civics EOC* (Grade 7, required to pass before HS graduation)

**Passing Requirement:**
- Level 3 (satisfactory) or higher
- EOC score counts as 30% of final course grade
- Concordant scores (SAT/ACT) may substitute for some EOCs

---

## Florida Graduation Pathways

**Scholar Designation:**
- Complete curriculum requirements for a Florida Academic Scholars (FAS) award (Bright Futures scholarship)
- Requires 3 math including Algebra II, 3 science, 2 world language, etc.

**Merit Designation:**
- Earn industry certification OR complete CTE pathway
- Attain minimum 3.0 GPA

**College and Career Ready:**
- Pass Postsecondary Education Readiness Test (PERT) OR
- Earn college credit through AP, AICE, IB, or dual enrollment

---

## State-Specific Considerations

**B.E.S.T. Standards (Benchmarks for Excellent Student Thinking):**
- Replaced Florida Standards (which were based on Common Core)
- Covers ELA, Math, Science, Social Studies

**Civics Literacy:**
- Required Civics course in Grade 7 with EOC assessment
- Must pass before high school graduation

**Financial Literacy:**
- Integrated into Economics course (0.5 credit)

**Holocaust Education:**
- Required instruction (part of social studies)

**African American History:**
- Required instruction (part of US History)

---

## Florida State Assessment System

**Florida Assessment of Student Thinking (FAST):**
- ELA and Math assessments (Grades 9-10)
- Used for school accountability, not graduation requirement

**EOC Assessments:**
- Algebra I, Geometry, Biology I, US History (required for graduation)
- Civics EOC (Grade 7, required before HS graduation)

---

## College Admission (Florida Public Universities)

**Minimum Admission Requirements:**
- 18 academic credits (specific distribution: 4 English, 4 Math including Algebra II, 3 Science including 2 lab, 3 Social Studies, 2 World Language, 2 electives)
- Minimum GPA (varies by institution)
- SAT/ACT optional at some institutions

**Bright Futures Scholarship (Florida Residents):**
- Florida Academic Scholars (FAS): 100% tuition + fees
- Florida Medallion Scholars (FMS): 75% tuition + fees
- **Requirements:** Community service hours, GPA, test scores, rigorous coursework

**Recommended Preparation:**
- Complete 18+ academic credits
- 4 years Math through Pre-Calculus or higher
- 3+ years Science (Biology, Chemistry, Physics)
- 2+ years World Language (same language)
- Honors, AP, AICE, IB, or dual enrollment courses

## Quick Reference

**Every Florida HS Material Must:**
- Align to B.E.S.T. Standards (ELA, Math, Science, Social Studies)
- Prepare for EOC assessments (Algebra I, Geometry, Biology I, US History)
- Include Economics with Financial Literacy content
- Support ESOL/WIDA for English Learners
- Address Florida-specific content (Holocaust education, African American history, civics)

**Key Point:** Florida requires EOC passage and EOC scores count as 30% of course grade. Materials must prepare students for high-stakes EOC assessments while building college and career readiness.
