# Houston ISD Curriculum Requirements
**District:** Houston ISD
**Applies To:** All K-8 instructional materials

---

## Overview

Houston ISD priorities beyond Texas state standards.

---

## 1. College and Career Readiness

**Vision:** Every student graduates ready for college and career

**K-8 Foundations:**
- Rigorous TEKS-aligned academics
- Critical thinking and problem-solving
- Career awareness (Houston industries)
- Growth mindset and perseverance

---

## 2. Houston Context

**Connect to Local Industries:**
- Energy (oil, gas, renewables)
- Space exploration (NASA)
- Healthcare and medical research (Texas Medical Center)
- Technology and innovation
- International trade (Port of Houston)

**Connect to Local Culture:**
- Diverse international communities
- Immigration stories
- Houston as energy capital

---

## 3. Data-Driven Instruction

**Houston ISD Assessment System:**
- STAAR (state test) preparation
- District benchmarks
- Formative assessments

**Materials Should:**
- Include embedded assessments
- Provide data reports
- Support intervention/differentiation
