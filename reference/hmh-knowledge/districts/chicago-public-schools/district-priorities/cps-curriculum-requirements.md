# CPS Curriculum Requirements and Priorities
**District:** Chicago Public Schools
**Applies To:** All K-8 instructional materials

---

## Overview

CPS district priorities beyond Illinois state standards, focused on equity, college readiness, and Chicago identity.

---

## 1. CPS Framework for Teaching (Danielson-Based)

**CPS uses Danielson Framework** for instruction and evaluation

**4 Domains:**
1. Planning and Preparation
2. Classroom Environment
3. Instruction
4. Professional Responsibilities

**Materials Should Support:**
- Rigorous, standards-aligned objectives (Domain 1)
- Positive, culturally responsive environment (Domain 2)
- Engaging, differentiated instruction (Domain 3)
- Assessment and data use (Domain 4)

---

## 2. STAR Framework (College/Career Readiness)

**Students Today Are Ready (STAR)**

**K-8 Focus:**
- Academic foundations (literacy, numeracy)
- 21st-century skills (collaboration, critical thinking, communication, creativity)
- Career exploration
- College-going culture
- Algebra by 8th grade

**Materials Must:**
- Build rigorous academic skills
- Connect to careers and real-world
- Include college/career vocabulary
- Develop growth mindset

---

## 3. Social-Emotional Learning (SEL)

**CPS adopts CASEL Framework**

**5 Competencies (integrated in all subjects):**
1. Self-Awareness
2. Self-Management
3. Social Awareness
4. Relationship Skills
5. Responsible Decision-Making

**Materials Should:**
- Include collaborative learning
- Provide reflection opportunities
- Foster empathy and perspective-taking
- Support positive classroom culture

---

## 4. Racial Equity and Social Justice

**CPS Priority:** Anti-racist, culturally sustaining curriculum

**In Materials:**
- Diverse representation (90%+ students of color)
- Multiple perspectives on history
- Critical analysis of systems and power
- Civic action opportunities
- Asset-based view of communities

**Examples:**
- Chicago civil rights history
- Great Migration
- Labor movements
- Environmental justice
- Economic equity themes

---

## 5. CPS Local Assessments

**District Assessment System:**
- NWEA MAP (3x per year, K-8)
- CPS common assessments
- Performance tasks
- Formative assessments

**Materials Should:**
- Align to IAR (state) and NWEA MAP (district)
- Include embedded assessments
- Provide data dashboards
- Support intervention/differentiation
