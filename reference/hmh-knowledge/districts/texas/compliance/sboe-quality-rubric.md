# Texas SBOE Quality Rubric - Mathematics K-12
**Source:** imra25-math-k12-sboe-approved-quality-rubric.pdf
**Approved:** November 22, 2024 by State Board of Education
**Updated:** November 26, 2024

---

## Overview

The Texas State Board of Education approved quality rubric defines 6 major dimensions of quality for mathematics instructional materials. All Into Math TX content must meet these criteria for state adoption.

**6 Quality Dimensions:**
1. Intentional Instructional Design
2. Progress Monitoring
3. Supports for All Learners
4. Depth and Coherence of Key Concepts
5. Balance of Conceptual and Procedural Understanding
6. Productive Struggle

---

- **Location:** Included in lessons already

**6.1b - Multiple Solution Paths** ⚠️ NEEDS ENHANCEMENT

**6.1c - Do, Write, Discuss Math**
- ✅ Designed to require students to make sense of mathematics through multiple opportunities to:

**6.2b - Explanatory Feedback**
