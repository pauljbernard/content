# CPS English Learner Framework
**District:** Chicago Public Schools
**English Learners:** 75,000+ (21% of enrollment)
**Languages:** 150+ languages, top: Spanish, Arabic, Polish, Chinese, Urdu
**Assessment:** WIDA ACCESS for ELLs

---

## Overview

CPS serves 75,000+ English Learners speaking 150+ home languages. CPS uses **WIDA** (World-Class Instructional Design and Assessment) framework with CPS-specific supports.

**See:** `/districts/illinois/language/illinois-el-standards.md` for complete WIDA guide (Illinois is WIDA consortium leader)

---

## WIDA 6 Proficiency Levels

**CPS follows WIDA's 6 levels:**
1. Entering
2. Emerging
3. Developing
4. Expanding
5. Bridging
6. Reaching

**Comprehensive WIDA guidance available in Illinois state file** (95%+ reuse)

---

## CPS-Specific EL Supports

### Transitional Bilingual Education (TBE):
- Instruction in home language and English
- Gradual transition to English
- Available in Spanish, Arabic, Polish, Chinese, others
- Maintains biliteracy as goal

### Bilingual Programs:
- Dual Language (two-way immersion)
- Transitional Bilingual (one-way)
- English as a Second Language (ESL)

### Newcomer Programs:
- Support for recently arrived immigrants/refugees
- Intensive English instruction
- Cultural orientation
- Trauma-informed supports

### Home Language Support:
- Bilingual materials in top languages
- Translation and interpretation
- Family engagement in home languages
- Community partnerships
