# North Dakota Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Public Instruction Requirements**
**Source:** North Dakota DPI
**Applies To:** All North Dakota instructional materials

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | North Dakota |
|--------|--------------|
| **Content Standards** | ND Content Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Science Standards** | ND Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | ND State Assessment (NDSA) |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## North Dakota Content Standards

**Mathematics:** ND Math Standards (based on CCSS-M, 95%+ aligned)
**ELA:** ND ELA Standards (based on CCSS-ELA)
**Science:** ND Science Standards (based on NGSS)
