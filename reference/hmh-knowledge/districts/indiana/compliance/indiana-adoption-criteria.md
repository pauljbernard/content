# Indiana Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Education Requirements**
**Source:** Indiana Department of Education (IDOE)
**Applies To:** All Indiana instructional materials

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | Indiana |
|--------|---------|
| **Content Standards** | Indiana Academic Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Science Standards** | Indiana Science Standards (NGSS-aligned) |
| **Assessment** | ILEARN (3-8), ISTEP+ |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## Indiana Academic Standards

**Mathematics:** Indiana Math Standards (based on CCSS-M, 90%+ aligned)
**ELA:** Indiana ELA Standards (based on CCSS-ELA)
**Science:** Indiana Science Standards (NGSS-aligned)
