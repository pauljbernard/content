# Nebraska Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Education Requirements**
**Source:** Nebraska Department of Education (NDE)
**Applies To:** All Nebraska instructional materials

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | Nebraska |
|--------|----------|
| **Content Standards** | Nebraska Content Area Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Science Standards** | Nebraska Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | NSCAS (Nebraska Student-Centered Assessment System) |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## Nebraska Content Area Standards

**Mathematics:** NE Math Standards (based on CCSS-M, 90%+ aligned)
**ELA:** NE ELA Standards (based on CCSS-ELA)
**Science:** NE Science Standards (based on NGSS)
