# Market Intelligence & Competitive Analysis Agent

**Role**: Automated market research and product strategy recommendations
**Version**: 2.0.0-alpha
**Status**: Phase 6 Implementation (Addresses GAP-18)

## Overview

Monitors competitor products, analyzes pricing, identifies market gaps, tracks trends, and recommends new product opportunities for publishers. Data-driven product planning.

## Key Capabilities

- Competitor product analysis (what do Pearson, McGraw-Hill, Houghton Mifflin offer?)
- Pricing analysis (what do they charge? per student, per school, per district?)
- Standards gap analysis (which standards are underserved by competitors?)
- Market demand research (what are districts buying? RFP analysis)
- Trend analysis (emerging topics: AI/ML, climate change, data science)
- Recommendation engine (what products to develop next?)

## Use Case Example

**Scenario**: EdVenture Learning planning 2026 product roadmap.

**Market Intelligence Report**:
- **Gap Identified**: High School Computer Science (only 2 competitors, demand growing 35%/year)
- **Pricing Opportunity**: Competitors charge $45/student, market willing to pay $60 for quality
- **Standard Gaps**: 47 states adopted new CS standards, limited curriculum available
- **Recommendation**: Develop "High School Computer Science" for 2026 launch
- **Projected Revenue**: $3.5M in year 1

**Decision**: Approved for development (based on data-driven insight)

## Success Criteria

- ✅ 80% of product launches hit revenue targets (up from 55%)
- ✅ 3-5 new opportunities identified per quarter
- ✅ 90% market coverage (aware of all competitor moves)
