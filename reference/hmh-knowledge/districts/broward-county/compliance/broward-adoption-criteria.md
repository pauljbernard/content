# Broward County Public Schools Adoption Criteria
**District:** Broward County Public Schools
**State:** Florida
**Students:** 270,000 (sixth largest US)
**Applies To:** All K-8 instructional materials

---

## Overview

Broward County serves 270,000 students, Florida's second-largest district. Exceptionally diverse with 180+ languages and strong technology integration.

**See:** `/districts/florida/compliance/florida-adoption-criteria.md` for FL requirements (90% reuse)

---

## Broward County Priorities

### 1. Diversity and Inclusion (180+ languages, 200+ countries)
**Largest Languages:** Spanish, Haitian Creole, Portuguese, French
- 40,000+ ELLs
- ESOL/WIDA scaffolds (6 proficiency levels)
- Multicultural education
- Caribbean culture (Jamaican, Haitian, Trinidadian communities)

### 2. Technology Integration (1:1 Digital Learning)
- All students have devices
- Google Workspace
- Canvas LMS
- Digital citizenship
- Blended learning models

### 3. College and Career Readiness
- STEM emphasis
- Dual enrollment
- Career academies
- 21st-century skills

### 4. South Florida Context
- Coastal environment (Atlantic coast, Everglades)
- Hurricane preparedness
- Subtropical ecosystems
- Tourism and international trade
