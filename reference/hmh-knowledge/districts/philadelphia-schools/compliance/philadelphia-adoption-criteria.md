# School District of Philadelphia Adoption Criteria
**District:** School District of Philadelphia
**State:** Pennsylvania
**Students:** 200,000
**Applies To:** All K-8 instructional materials

---

## Overview

Philadelphia serves 200,000 students, the largest district in Pennsylvania. Diverse urban environment with strong emphasis on literacy and social-emotional learning.

**See:** `/districts/pennsylvania/compliance/pennsylvania-adoption-criteria.md` for PA requirements (90% reuse)

---

## Philadelphia Schools Priorities

### 1. Literacy Focus (Read by 4th Grade)
- District-wide literacy initiative
- Early literacy screening (K-2)
- Intervention for struggling readers
- Literacy across all subjects (math, science, social studies)
- Book access and libraries

### 2. Multilingual Learners (15,000+ MLLs, 100+ languages)
**Top Languages:** Spanish, Chinese, Vietnamese, Arabic, French/Haitian Creole
- PA ELD scaffolds
- ESL programs
- Bilingual supports
- Newcomer programs
- Cultural responsiveness (African, Asian, Latino communities)

### 3. Social-Emotional Learning (CASEL)
- Trauma-informed practices
- Restorative justice
- Positive school climate
- Student wellness and support

### 4. Philadelphia Context
- Philadelphia history (Revolutionary War, Constitution, civil rights)
- Urban environment and challenges
- Cultural institutions (museums, historical sites)
- Diverse neighborhoods
