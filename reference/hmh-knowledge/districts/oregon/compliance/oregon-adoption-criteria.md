# Oregon Adoption Criteria
**State Board of Education Requirements**
**Source:** Oregon Department of Education (ODE)
**Applies To:** All Oregon instructional materials (all subjects)

---

## Overview

**Oregon uses local control for curriculum adoption** with state guidance through ODE. All materials must align to Oregon State Standards and support English Learners.

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | Oregon |
|--------|--------|
| **Content Standards** | Oregon State Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | OR ELD Standards (state-specific) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Compliance** | ODE guidance |
| **Science Standards** | Oregon Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | OSAS (Oregon Statewide Assessment System) |
| **EL Assessment** | ELPA21 assessment |

---

## Oregon State Standards

**Mathematics:** Oregon Mathematics Standards (based on CCSS-M, 95%+ identical)

**ELA:** Oregon English Language Arts Standards (based on CCSS-ELA)

**Science:** Oregon Science Standards (based on NGSS)

---

## English Learner Support

**Oregon uses state-specific ELD standards:**
- Oregon English Language Development Standards
- Similar framework to WA ELD
- ELPA21 assessment (same as Washington)
- 5 proficiency levels

**See:** `/districts/oregon/language/oregon-eld-standards.md` for complete guidance
