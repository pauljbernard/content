# Minnesota Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Education Requirements**
**Source:** Minnesota Department of Education (MDE)
**Applies To:** All Minnesota instructional materials

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | Minnesota |
|--------|-----------|
| **Content Standards** | Minnesota Academic Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Science Standards** | Minnesota Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | MCA (Minnesota Comprehensive Assessments) |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## Minnesota Academic Standards

**Mathematics:** MN Math Standards (based on CCSS-M, 90%+ aligned)
**ELA:** MN ELA Standards (based on CCSS-ELA)
**Science:** MN Science Standards (based on NGSS)
