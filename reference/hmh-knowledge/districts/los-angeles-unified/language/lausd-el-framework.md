# LAUSD English Learner Framework
**District:** Los Angeles Unified School District
**English Learners:** 100,000+ (17% of enrollment)
**Languages:** 90+ languages, top 6: Spanish, Armenian, Korean, Filipino, Chinese, Arabic
**Assessment:** ELPAC (English Language Proficiency Assessments for California)

---

## Overview

LAUSD serves over 100,000 English Learners across 90+ home languages. The district uses CA ELD Standards (5 proficiency levels) with additional LAUSD-specific supports.

**Core Principle:** Designated ELD + Integrated ELD across all subjects

---

## CA ELD Proficiency Levels (5 Levels)

**See:** `/districts/california/language/california-eld-standards.md` for full CA ELD framework

**LAUSD uses CA's 5 levels:**
1. Emerging
2. Expanding  
3. Bridging
4. (Approaching proficiency)
5. (At proficiency)

---

## LAUSD-Specific EL Supports

### Designated ELD (30-45 min daily):
- Explicit English language instruction
- Focus on CA ELD standards
- Small group or whole class
- ELD curriculum materials

### Integrated ELD (All day, all subjects):
- Language development in Math, Science, Social Studies
- Academic language scaffolds
- MLRs in Math, literacy routines in all subjects
- Content + language objectives

### Home Language Support:
- Bilingual instruction where available
- Primary Language Support materials
- Dual Language programs (50+ schools)
- Translation and interpretation services

### SDAIE Strategies:
- Specially Designed Academic Instruction in English
- Visual supports, graphic organizers
- Collaborative learning structures
- Comprehensible input techniques

---

## Top Languages - Translation Requirements

**Priority for bilingual materials:**
1. Spanish (70%+ of ELs)
2. Armenian
