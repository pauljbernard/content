# Ohio Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Education Requirements**
**Source:** Ohio Department of Education (ODE)
**Applies To:** All Ohio instructional materials (all subjects)

---

## Overview

**Ohio uses local control for curriculum adoption** with state oversight through ODE. All materials must align to Ohio's Learning Standards and support English Learners.

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | Ohio |
|--------|------|
| **Content Standards** | Ohio's Learning Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Compliance** | ODE guidance |
| **Science Standards** | Ohio's Learning Standards for Science (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | Ohio State Tests (OST) |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## Ohio's Learning Standards

**Mathematics:** Ohio's Learning Standards for Mathematics (based on CCSS-M, 95%+ identical)

**ELA:** Ohio's Learning Standards for English Language Arts (based on CCSS-ELA)

**Science:** Ohio's Learning Standards for Science (based on NGSS with Ohio Model Curriculum)

---

## English Learner Support

**Ohio is a WIDA consortium member:**
- WIDA ELD Standards
- ACCESS for ELLs
- 6 proficiency levels

**See:** `/districts/illinois/language/illinois-el-standards.md` for complete WIDA guidance
