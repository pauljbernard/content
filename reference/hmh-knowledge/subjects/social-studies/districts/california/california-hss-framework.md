# California History-Social Science Framework
**California Department of Education**
**Source:** California SBE
**Applies To:** K-12 Social Studies (California only)

---

## Overview

**California uses the History-Social Science (HSS) Framework** organized around chronological and thematic study with emphasis on democratic values and civic participation.

---

## CA HSS Organization

**K-3:** Learning and Working Now and Long Ago
**4:** California History and Geography
**5:** United States History and Geography
**6:** World History and Geography: Ancient Civilizations
**7:** World History and Geography: Medieval and Early Modern Times
**8:** United States History and Geography: Growth and Conflict

---

## CA-Specific Features

**Grade 4:** Required California history and geography
**Civic Learning:** Emphasis on civic knowledge and participation
**ELD Integration:** Built-in scaffolds for English Learners

**See:** `/districts/california/language/eld-alignment.md` for ELD integration
