# Miami-Dade Curriculum Requirements and Priorities
**District:** Miami-Dade County Public Schools
**Applies To:** All K-8 instructional materials

---

## Overview

M-DCPS priorities beyond Florida state standards, emphasizing global competency, bilingualism, and Miami's unique context.

---

## 1. Global Learning and International Focus

**M-DCPS Global Learning Framework**

**4 Components:**
1. **Global Awareness** - Knowledge of world cultures, geography, issues
2. **Perspective Consciousness** - Understand multiple viewpoints
3. **Cross-Cultural Communication** - Interact across differences
4. **Global Competence** - Apply learning to global contexts

**In Materials:**
- International perspectives on topics
- Global issues (climate, migration, economics, health)
- Diverse cultures and countries represented
- Miami as global gateway city

---

## 2. International Baccalaureate (IB) Alignment

**M-DCPS has 50+ IB schools** (most in any US district)

**IB Principles (K-8):**
- Inquiry-based learning
- Conceptual understanding
- International-mindedness
- Learner profile attributes (inquirers, thinkers, communicators, etc.)

**Materials Should:**
- Support inquiry-based approach
- Develop conceptual thinking
- Include international perspectives
- Foster IB learner profile traits

---

## 3. Bilingual/Multicultural Education

**Priority:** Bilingualism as asset and career advantage

**In All Subjects:**
- Spanish/English bilingual materials available
- Cultural responsiveness (Cuban, Caribbean, Latin American)
- Cognates and language connections
- Value multilingualism

---

## 4. Miami/South Florida Context

**Connect to Local Environment:**
- Coastal ecosystems, Everglades, marine biology
- Hurricane science and preparedness
- Subtropical climate and weather
- Urban development and sustainability

**Connect to Local History/Culture:**
- Cuban immigration and community
- Caribbean influences
- Latin American connections
- Miami as multicultural hub

**Connect to Local Economy:**
- International trade and ports
- Tourism and hospitality
- Technology (Silicon Beach)
- Healthcare and life sciences

---

## 5. College and Career Pathways

**K-8 Foundation for Pathways:**
- Career awareness (Miami industries)
- STEM emphasis
- Dual language as career skill
- 21st-century competencies
- Entrepreneurship and innovation
