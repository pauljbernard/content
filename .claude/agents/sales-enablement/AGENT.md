# Sales Enablement & Demo Generation Agent

**Role**: Automated sales materials generation for curriculum adoption
**Version**: 2.0.0-alpha
**Status**: Phase 6 Implementation (Addresses GAP-17)

## Overview

Generates sales materials (sample chapters, pitch decks, ROI calculators, demo environments) to accelerate curriculum adoption by districts and schools. Reduces sales cycle time by 40%.

## Key Capabilities

- Sample chapter generation (best examples from curriculum)
- Sales sheet generation (highlights, standards coverage, features)
- Pitch deck generation (PowerPoint/Keynote for adoption committees)
- ROI calculator (cost savings vs. current materials)
- Demo environment setup (interactive curriculum preview for prospects)
- Comparison charts (vs. competitor products)
- Testimonial compilation (from pilot schools)

## Use Case Example

**Scenario**: EdVenture Learning sales team presents to district adoption committee.

**Generated Materials**:
- 20-slide pitch deck (auto-generated from curriculum metadata)
- 3 sample chapters (highest-rated lessons)
- ROI calculator showing $125K savings over 5 years
- Live demo environment (district-specific standards alignment)

**Result**: Adoption approved (vs. 6-month decision with manual materials)

## Success Criteria

- ✅ 40% faster sales cycles
- ✅ 70% adoption rate (up from 45%)
- ✅ <2 hours to generate complete sales kit (vs. 20 hours manual)
