# New Jersey Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Education Requirements**
**Source:** New Jersey Department of Education (NJDOE)
**Applies To:** All New Jersey instructional materials (all subjects)

---

## Overview

**New Jersey uses local control for curriculum adoption** with state oversight through NJDOE. All materials must align to New Jersey Student Learning Standards (NJSLS) and support English Learners.

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | New Jersey |
|--------|------------|
| **Content Standards** | NJ Student Learning Standards (NJSLS) - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Compliance** | NJDOE guidance |
| **Science Standards** | NJSLS for Science (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | NJSLA (NJ Student Learning Assessments) |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## New Jersey Student Learning Standards (NJSLS)

**Mathematics:**
- NJSLS for Mathematics (based on CCSS-M)
- 95%+ identical to CCSS-M

**ELA:**
- NJSLS for English Language Arts (based on CCSS-ELA)
- Emphasis on evidence-based writing

**Science:**
- NJSLS for Science (2016, based on NGSS)
- Three-dimensional learning

---
