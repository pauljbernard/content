# Iowa Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Education Requirements**
**Source:** Iowa Department of Education
**Applies To:** All Iowa instructional materials

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | Iowa |
|--------|------|
| **Content Standards** | Iowa Core Standards - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Science Standards** | Iowa Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | Iowa Statewide Assessment of Student Progress (ISASP) |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## Iowa Core Standards

**Mathematics:** Iowa Core Math (based on CCSS-M, 95%+ aligned)
**ELA:** Iowa Core ELA (based on CCSS-ELA)
**Science:** Iowa Science Standards (based on NGSS)
