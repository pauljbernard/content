# Maine Adoption Criteria
**State Department of Education Requirements**
**Source:** Maine DOE
**Applies To:** All Maine instructional materials

---

## Key Facts

| Aspect | Maine |
|--------|-------|
| **Content Standards** | Maine Learning Results - CCSS-based |
| **Language Standards** | English Learners (WIDA consortium) |
| **Adoption Process** | Local district control |
| **Science Standards** | ME Science Standards (NGSS-based) |
| **Assessment** | ME Through-Year Assessment |
| **EL Assessment** | ACCESS for ELLs (WIDA) |

---

## Maine Learning Results

**Mathematics:** ME Math Standards (based on CCSS-M, 95%+ aligned)
**ELA:** ME ELA Standards (based on CCSS-ELA)
**Science:** ME Science Standards (based on NGSS)
